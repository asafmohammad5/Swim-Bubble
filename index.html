Swim Bubble

Welcome

Swim Bubble was inspired by the well known game Flappy Bird, which was created by Dong Nguyen in 2013.

Swim Bubble was created by Asaf Mohammad. The source code for this game can be found at Github.

How to Play

The player needs to get the big air bubbles in order to stay alive. By left clicking with the mouse the dolphin will move up, and not clicking at all the dolphin will begin to move down. Avoid the red coral at the bottom, dont swim to far up, and dont run out of breath!
